Purpose of the Project

Tools Used

HTML | CSS | Threejs | OpenGl | Git

In a team of three, we created a three.js platform game with infinite number of suspended platforms of different height and different moments The view of the gameplay is in the third-person perspective of the player. Features such as collision detection, level of details control, procedural modelling and motion are implemented in the game. Use WASD to move forward, left, right or backward respectively.

algorithms used include ray-casting and kinematics (dynamics).

Design and Development Processes

Game in play with more platforms added as you climb up to infinity

The game is as interesting as it gets. The goal is to get the highest height possible. There are cheatsheet for those less confident of achieving greater height by toggling which practically increase their chances of how farer they can jump.
